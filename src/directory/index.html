Mozilla Games Directory

A collection of outstanding HTML5 games in the wild.
  1. Full Screen Mario
    by Diogenesthecynic
    An HTML5 remake of the original Super Mario Brothers.
  2. Banana Bread
    by Mozilla
    BananaBread is a 3D first-person shooter based on Cube 2: Sauerbraten.
  3. Bastion
    by Supergiant Games
    Bastion is an original action RPG featuring a reactive narrator who marks your every move, lush hand-painted 2D artwork, and tight responsive gameplay that rewards playing with finesse.
  4. Dungeon Fury
    by Dungeon Fury
    Beware of the brutish Orcs in this awesome 3D action game that tests your reflexes to the very limit!
  5. HexGL
    by BKCore
    HexGL is a futuristic, fast-paced racing game built by Thibaut Despoulain using HTML5, Javascript and WebGL and a tribute to the original Wipeout and F-Zero series.
  6. Dead Trigger II
    by Madfinger Games
    Enter a world where humanity is engaged in the ultimate battle for survival in the face of a global Zombie apocalypse.
  7. Pokemon
    by Nintendo
    Full Screen Pokemon is an open source HTML5 remake of the original Pokemon games. You can play the original two generations (Red/Blue through Crystal), literally billions of procedurally generated maps, or create your own using the level editor.
  8. Monster Madness
    by Playverse
    Four friends get together for a party while the parents are away, but somebody â€¦ or something, is about to crash the party.
  9. Quake 3 Arena
    by TojiCode
    Welcome to the Arena, where high-ranking warriors are transformed into spineless mush. Abandoning every ounce of common sense and any trace of doubt, you lunge onto a stage of harrowing landscapes and veiled abysses.
  10. Roller Coaster VR
    by Google Chrome
    Buckle up for a ride in a roller coaster. Virtual reality at its finest.
  11. Urban Galaxy
    by Urban Galaxy
    Urban Galaxy is an HTML5 3D real-time MMORPG set in a futuristic Megapolis. Choose your career and play in rich urbanscapes with your friends.
